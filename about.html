About Biruh

I'm a graphic designer based in Addis Ababa with a background in architecture — which means I think about space, structure, and hierarchy before I think about decoration. That discipline shows up in everything I make.

Currently Senior Graphic Designer at Ayn Studios, where I work across brand identity, campaign design, and social media production. In parallel, I take on independent projects — brands that need an identity built from scratch, NGOs that need stories told visually, and businesses entering new markets that need to look the part.

I've delivered a live law firm rebrand (MAP LLC, now at millionlegalservices.com), a coffee export brand identity, a mattress marketing campaign, and an original cartoon series for DEC Ethiopia — all as the sole creative on each project.

I take projects from brief to delivery, independently. If you have a brand that needs a face worth remembering, let's talk.
