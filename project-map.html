Brand Identity · 2024

MAP LLC — Full Brand Identity

Million Alemu & Partners Legal Services needed a complete brand identity built from zero. Sole designer from brief to final delivery — now live.

Add your best MAP LLC mockup here (hero.jpg)
The brief
MAP LLC (Million Alemu & Partners Legal Services) had no visual identity. They needed a complete brand — logo, color system, typography, and assets — to represent a serious law firm entering a competitive market.
The challenge
Law firm branding has to balance authority with approachability. Too corporate feels cold and distant. Too modern loses trust. Every decision had to serve that tension — credible, but human.
Logo design
Mark, wordmark & variations
The final logo system includes a primary lockup, a secondary horizontal version, and a standalone icon mark for small applications like favicons and stamps.

Color system
Authority and warmth in balance
Deep navy anchors the identity with gravitas. Gold brings warmth and signals heritage. Cream and charcoal provide clean, readable backgrounds for all documents and digital surfaces.

Typography
Serif for headings — communicates tradition and trustworthiness. Clean sans-serif for body copy — readable, modern, precise. A pairing that says: established, but not stuck in the past.
Brand applications
The identity in use
Business cards, letterhead, and digital assets all follow a strict grid system to ensure consistency regardless of who is applying the brand.
