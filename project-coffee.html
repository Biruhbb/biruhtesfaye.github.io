Brand Identity · Freelance · 2024

Assefa Yaara Coffee Export

A full brand identity for an Ethiopian coffee exporter entering the global market. Built independently from first sketch to final client presentation.

Add your best Coffee brand hero image here (hero.jpg)
The brief
Assefa Yaara needed a brand identity that would represent Ethiopian coffee heritage while being polished enough to compete in international export markets. No existing identity — everything from scratch.
The challenge
Ethiopian coffee carries deep cultural meaning. The brand had to feel rooted and authentic, not generic or imitative of Western coffee aesthetics — while still being accessible to global buyers and retailers.
Logo design
A mark rooted in origin
The logo balances a distinctive wordmark with an icon mark that references Ethiopian cultural visual language. Designed to work equally well on export packaging, certificates, and digital platforms.

Color & texture
Earthy, rich, and premium
The palette draws from the coffee bean itself — deep roast browns, warm terracotta, and natural cream. Colors that smell like coffee before you read the name.
