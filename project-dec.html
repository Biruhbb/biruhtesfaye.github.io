DEC Ethiopia — Cartoon Series

An original storytelling cartoon series for an NGO, developed independently from character design through to finished frames. Built to communicate DEC Ethiopia's mission to its audience.

Add your best finished illustration here (hero.jpg)
The brief
DEC Ethiopia needed a visual storytelling series that could communicate their NGO's mission in an engaging, accessible way — using illustration and narrative rather than text-heavy communications.
My role
Freelance illustrator and art director. Character design, visual style development, storyboarding, panel illustration, and final production — fully independent from brief to delivery.
Character design
From sketch to final characters
Characters were developed from initial rough sketches through refined character sheets, establishing personality, proportion, and expression range before a single panel was drawn.
